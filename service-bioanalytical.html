A study is only as reliable as the assay behind it — method development and validation is where data quality is won or lost.

We develop, validate, and run methods with the sensitivity, selectivity, and stability coverage your program requires, and document them to withstand audit.

Validated to the right guideline, first time

Methods are developed against the target submission (ICH M10 for regulated bioanalysis), so validation packages are accepted without rework.

What we deliver

Our bioanalytical services span method development through regulated sample analysis and biomarker support.

Method development
LC–MS/MS and ligand-binding (ELISA) assay development for small molecules, peptides, and biologics.
Method validation
Accuracy, precision, selectivity, linearity, LLOQ, recovery, and stability under ICH Q2 (R1) / M10.
Sample analysis
GLP-compliant study-sample analysis with incurred-sample reanalysis where required.
Stability studies
Bench-top, freeze–thaw, long-term, and stock-solution stability per ICH Q1A–E.
Impurity profiling
Identification and quantitation of impurities and degradants.
Biomarker support
Fit-for-purpose biomarker method qualification for PK/PD and clinical endpoints.

How an engagement runs

A consistent five-stage flow keeps every engagement aligned with regulatory intent — from feasibility through a decision-ready report.

01 · Scoping

Feasibility & gap analysis

Map existing data to your target submission; identify gaps, endpoints, and study-design options.

02 · Design

Protocol & plan

Draft an OECD/ICH-aligned protocol with a statistical/analysis plan and clear acceptance criteria.

03 · Execution

Conduct

Run the work under quality oversight at accredited partner facilities with contemporaneous data capture.

04 · Analysis

Review & interpret

QA-cleared data, biostatistics where relevant, and scientific interpretation — not just raw delivery.

05 · Reporting

Decision-ready report

A structured report and data package prepared with audit and agency review in mind.

Quality & data integrity

Work is conducted under quality-managed SOPs with ALCOA+ data-integrity principles. Raw data is captured contemporaneously, archived, and made available for sponsor and agency audit.

  • ISO 9001 quality-management system across the lab's operations.
  • Independent review of protocols, conduct, and final reports before release.
  • Traceable records — every result traces back to an explicit method and standard.
  • Confidential by default — mutual NDA available within 24 hours of request.
