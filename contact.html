Frequently asked

What to expect when you reach out.

No sales funnels, no automated triage. Your first reply is from a scientist who can speak to the study itself.

Response time
We respond within two working days with a scoping note. For urgent feasibility queries, mention "urgent" in your subject and we'll prioritise.
Confidentiality
A mutual NDA can be in place within 24 hours of request. We treat all sponsor information as confidential by default — even before formal agreements.
First meeting
A 30-minute scientific call with the relevant service lead — not a sales rep. Bring your TPP, target indication, and any prior data; we'll bring study design ideas.
Quotation
After the scoping call, you receive a written proposal with study design, timeline, deliverables, and pricing — typically within five working days.
Onboarding
From contract signature to first dose is typically 4 – 6 weeks, depending on ethics-committee approval, protocol complexity, and test article availability.
