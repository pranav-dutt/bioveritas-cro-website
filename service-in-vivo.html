The right in vivo model turns a hypothesis into a development decision — provided it is designed around a translational question, not a template.

We select models for translational relevance, power studies appropriately, and interpret efficacy alongside exposure so results inform dose selection downstream.

Efficacy read next to exposure

Coupling efficacy endpoints with PK sampling lets us build PK/PD relationships that guide dose selection for later toxicology and clinical work.

What we deliver

We run disease-relevant efficacy and mechanism studies, individually or as an integrated proof-of-concept package.

Efficacy models
Disease-relevant rodent and large-animal models across oncology, metabolic, CNS, inflammation, and dermatology.
PK/PD studies
Exposure–response designs linking pharmacokinetics to pharmacodynamic endpoints.
Proof of concept
Go/no-go studies designed to answer a specific translational question with adequate power.
Biomarker & endpoint capture
Terminal and longitudinal endpoints — imaging, histology, and circulating biomarkers where relevant.
Model development
Adaptation or establishment of bespoke models where an off-the-shelf design will not answer the question.

Standards & methods

Studies run under ethics-committee approval with humane endpoints and welfare oversight, documented for downstream submission.

How an engagement runs

A consistent five-stage flow keeps every engagement aligned with regulatory intent — from feasibility through a decision-ready report.

01 · Scoping

Feasibility & gap analysis

Map existing data to your target submission; identify gaps, endpoints, and study-design options.

02 · Design

Protocol & plan

Draft an OECD/ICH-aligned protocol with a statistical/analysis plan and clear acceptance criteria.

03 · Execution

Conduct

Run the work under quality oversight at accredited partner facilities with contemporaneous data capture.

04 · Analysis

Review & interpret

QA-cleared data, biostatistics where relevant, and scientific interpretation — not just raw delivery.

05 · Reporting

Decision-ready report

A structured report and data package prepared with audit and agency review in mind.

Quality & data integrity

Work is conducted under quality-managed SOPs with ALCOA+ data-integrity principles. Raw data is captured contemporaneously, archived, and made available for sponsor and agency audit.

  • ISO 9001 quality-management system across the lab's operations.
  • Independent review of protocols, conduct, and final reports before release.
  • Traceable records — every result traces back to an explicit method and standard.
  • Confidential by default — mutual NDA available within 24 hours of request.
