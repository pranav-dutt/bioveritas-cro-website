Discovery is a filtering problem — the earlier you find the fatal flaw, the cheaper it is. We build screens that fail candidates fast and advance winners with evidence.

From primary screening through preformulation and prototype comparison, we generate the data that de-risks the transition into formal development.

Fail fast, advance with evidence

Screening cascades are designed so weak candidates drop out early and the survivors carry data that supports the next investment decision.

What we deliver

Our discovery and NPD-screening services support the path from hit to development-ready candidate.

High-throughput screening
Primary and secondary screening cascades against your target or endpoint.
Hit-to-lead support
Characterisation and prioritisation of hits to identify viable leads.
Preformulation
Solubility, stability, and compatibility profiling to guide formulation.
Prototype development
Early prototype preparation and characterisation for proof-of-concept.
Comparative analysis
Head-to-head evaluation against reference or competitor products.

Standards & methods

Screening and characterisation methods are qualified for the decision they support and documented for reproducibility.

How an engagement runs

A consistent five-stage flow keeps every engagement aligned with regulatory intent — from feasibility through a decision-ready report.

01 · Scoping

Feasibility & gap analysis

Map existing data to your target submission; identify gaps, endpoints, and study-design options.

02 · Design

Protocol & plan

Draft an OECD/ICH-aligned protocol with a statistical/analysis plan and clear acceptance criteria.

03 · Execution

Conduct

Run the work under quality oversight at accredited partner facilities with contemporaneous data capture.

04 · Analysis

Review & interpret

QA-cleared data, biostatistics where relevant, and scientific interpretation — not just raw delivery.

05 · Reporting

Decision-ready report

A structured report and data package prepared with audit and agency review in mind.

Quality & data integrity

Work is conducted under quality-managed SOPs with ALCOA+ data-integrity principles. Raw data is captured contemporaneously, archived, and made available for sponsor and agency audit.

  • ISO 9001 quality-management system across the lab's operations.
  • Independent review of protocols, conduct, and final reports before release.
  • Traceable records — every result traces back to an explicit method and standard.
  • Confidential by default — mutual NDA available within 24 hours of request.
